# Travel With Me

**Travel With Me** is a collaborative trip planning and expense management platform for groups who want to plan smarter, spend transparently, and preserve travel memories in one place.

It combines itinerary planning, shared cost tracking, invite-based collaboration, wishlist discovery, community conversations, and a visual trip story experience so every trip can be organized and memorable.

## Why Travel With Me

- Plan trips with your entire group in one shared workspace.
- Track real spending with clear split logic and settlement clarity.
- Capture places, photos, notes, and ratings as a living travel journal.
- Share a polished trip story page with friends and family.

## Core Features

- **Collaborative Trip Workspace**
  - Create and manage trips with title, cover image, timeline, start point, and transport details.
  - Track trip lifecycle with status flow: planning, started, ongoing, ended.
  - Keep all members aligned in one shared experience.

- **Fast Discovery on Trip Cards**
  - Open a right-side info drawer for each trip.
  - See user-friendly trip overview, activity highlights, and crew details.
  - Quick actions for Story, Community, and Join/Open directly from the drawer.

- **Advanced Group Expense Splitting**
  - Add expenses with multiple split types:
    - Equal
    - Exact Amount
    - Percentage
    - Shares
  - Categorize spending for better visibility.
  - Auto-calculate individual contributions and balances.

- **Smart Settlement Visibility**
  - Generate clear “who owes whom” outcomes from group expenses.
  - Reduce confusion and simplify reimbursement conversations.

- **Visited Places Journal**
  - Add visited places with date/day, notes, category tags, and media.
  - Rate places quickly with star-based rating.
  - Build a timeline of where the group went and what they experienced.

- **Trip Story Mode (Shareable)**
  - Beautiful story-style trip page with:
    - Hero visuals
    - Crew highlights
    - Journey timeline
    - Quick logistics summary
  - Shareable link for external viewing.

- **Trip Community (Shareable Page)**
  - Dedicated community page per trip that can be opened via external link.
  - Message-first threads with replies for group conversations.
  - Edit/delete support with soft-delete behavior to preserve conversation context.

- **Invite and Access Flow**
  - Invite users to trips with token-based links.
  - Join trip flow with clear access states (Open / Request / Requested).
  - Built for group coordination without messy manual onboarding.

- **Insightful Dashboard**
  - Visual expense breakdown by category.
  - Category labels integrated directly on chart bars for faster readability.
  - Member and trip metrics at a glance.
  - Better visibility for decision-making while traveling.

- **Wishlist + Personalized Profile**
  - Save trips to wishlist directly from home trip cards.
  - Profile tabs for Account, Wishlists, and Your Past Crews.
  - Reconnect with people you have traveled with before.

- **Modern Authentication and User Sync**
  - Clerk-based authentication experience.
  - Dedicated login/signup/profile routes.
  - Webhook-driven user sync into app database using `CLERK_SIGNING_SECRET`.

- **Polished Interaction UX**
  - Global click sound feedback across interactive components.
  - Context-aware trip actions (join flow for active trips, story flow for ended trips).
  - Cleaner, engaging cards and action chips across the app.

## Ideal Use Cases

- Friends planning weekend trips.
- Family vacations with shared budgets.
- Group tours where multiple people pay at different times.
- Travel creators who want a shareable trip story experience.
